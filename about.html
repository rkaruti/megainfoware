About Mega Infoware

Best in Finding Root Cause

Mega Infoware was founded with a simple objective - to become a leading off shore development center in this millennium. To achieve our objective we firmly believe that three things are essential - quality people, quality processes and continuous client relationship. From the very first day of inception the focus has been on getting the best talents in their respective fields and setting up quality business processes based on ISO 9001 model. We shall continue to strive in that direction till we meet our objective.

Mega Infoware has been a value added solution provider for client/ server and web-based solutions from the time of its inception.

There has been a consistent effort at Mega Infoware towards making it a Quality Information Technology Solutions provider. Information Technology forms an integral tool in the process of evolving the organization for the future challenges. In that direction we have adopted latest project Management skills and developed expert domain Knowledge, which is a must for successful execution of enterprise solutions.

With every new day the quest for acquiring new competencies continues forever. Searching, experimenting, innovating and learning. Moving ahead, shaping the future, and making competencies create opportunities.


Business Solutions

Mega Infoware is a company that believes "the Customer comes first!".

Our business model focuses on having long-term strategic relationships with clients. A significant portion of our revenues comes from repeating business. This is possible because we believe in working transparently with the clients and looking into their requirements.

A broad talent base of professionals in R&D, Corporate training, sales, marketing, support and administration complements a strong foundation in technology. This balance has been instrumental in the company's successful forays.

We at Mega Infoware believe in providing Quality Solutions and support, for any kind of job we undertake from our clients. Our main emphasis is to provide high return on investment for the "Business Critical Applications" in the shortest span of time. This is possible mainly because of our focus on Quality procedures and Methodologies, which are imbibed in our system. Motivated by the support of its clients all over India, Mega Infoware is soon gearing up for expansion in India as well as Abroad. Our primary goal is to help the organization and individuals we serve in business, industry, education and government to become the beneficiaries of advanced technology by providing software and services to help them meet their organizational goals.

Expertise: Mega infoware's Technical Training Services calls on the expertise and service of training engineers. All are experts in their respective disciplines, with detailed knowledge of product hardware, software, operations, installation, and maintenance as well as in-depth understanding of system architecture and design.

Expansive facilities equipped with state-of-the-art technology: Our training facilities includes up-to-date training rooms. All of our training rooms are adjacent to glassed-in laboratories and configured with complete product system mock ups that are fully under the instructor's control. This hands-on environment enables students to get a feel for the real-world operation of the system, and allows the instructor to create scenarios that bolster students' problem-solving capabilities and understanding of the intricacies of a complex system. While training in this controlled environment is preferable, Mega Infoware can accommodate training needs at customer sites for many product lines, using either customer equipment offline or, in some cases, a scaled-down mobile version from Mega Infoware.
