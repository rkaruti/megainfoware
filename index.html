Welcome to Mega Infoware !

About Us

Mega Infoware was founded with a simple objective - to become a leading off shore development center in this millennium. To achieve our objective we firmly believe that three things are essential - quality people, quality processes and continuous client relationships. From the very first day of inception the focus has been on getting the best talents in their respective fields and setting up quality business processes based on ISO 9001 model. We shall continue to strive in that direction till we meet our objective.
